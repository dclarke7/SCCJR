# The SCC JR of Legislation Database
This repository hosts "The Supreme Court of Canada Judicial Review of Legislation Database" by Dylan R. Clarke.
The database contains the universe decisions pertaining to the judicial review of legislation made by the Supreme Court of Canada from the inception of the Charter to present (1984-2023). Data are at the case- and justice-level. 
Please cite as "Dylan R. Clarke, The Supreme Court of Canada Judicial Review of Legislation Database, 1984-2023".
